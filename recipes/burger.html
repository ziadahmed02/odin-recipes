Homemade Burger Recipe

This one is my favorite

Ingredients

  • Brioche Buns
  • Beef Patty
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Honey Mustard Sauce

Steps

  1. On a searing hot pan, add the beef patty until medium rare
  2. Toast the buns on low heat after removing the patty
  3. assemble all the ingredients
  4. Don't forget the sauce!
