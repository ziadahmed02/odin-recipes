Fried Chicken

Homemade Fried Chicken Recipe

This recipe is very easy to make when in a hurry

Ingredients

  • Chicken Pieces
  • Wet Batter
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Submerge the chicken pieces in the wet batter
  2. Add them to the flour so they would develop the crust
  3. Add them to hot oil for about 15 mins
